voting or online voting is a system of voting where people can vote directly from their mobile, anytime and from anywhere


Mobile voting is a revolution which is disrupting the traditional election systems with the power of the internet.

>Key elements of mobile voting are

Voter can vote directly from their mobile phone: A voter need not travel to a booth to vote. A voter does not need any voting equipment. Voter's personal mobile phone is his voting equipment. The authentication of the voter happens online and without any human intervention. Vote from anywhere: Voter can vote from anywhere. Voting is not confined to a booth or a location.
Vote anytime: Voter can vote anytime during the duration of the election. Voting can happen 24*7. Voter need not wait in a queue or wait for the booths to open. Powered by internet: Mobile voting is powered by internet. That means the voting transactions happens in real-time. The whole process of voter authentication, voting and result counting is automatic and without any human interference. The process of mobile voting is very simple, transparent, secure, cost efficient and time efficient.

Steps in mobile voting:

Voter gets a SMS notification with an election link.
Voter clicks on the link which takes him to the login page of the website.
Voter logs in using his mobile number and One Time Password (OTP) delivered to him via SMS. For critical elections, there is the option of multiple layers of authentication including Aadhaar biometric / OTP, picture capture, email OTP etc.
The ballot page opens up with candidate names and pictures. Voter selects the candidate of his choice by touch.
Voter then presses the DONE button. The vote is recorded, and the voter gets a confirmation message via SMS.
Result is automatically computed and published instantly after voting closes
