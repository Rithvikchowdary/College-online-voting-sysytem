Our Principles

  • 1

    BALLOT BOX

    • No election official, process, ballot, or voting equipment shall make it possible to determine or modify by any means, direct or indirect, how an individual votes in any race, on any issue, for any party, or any candidate. • No election equipment or process shall make it possible by any means for any voter or election official to market a vote or ballots to any individual, group, or party. • No election process or equipment shall make it possible to influence a voter as to who or what they vote for at any time while the ballot is in possession of the voter.
  • 2

    ONE MAN,ONE VOTE

    • Each voter must be individually identified by the most reliable means available, preferably face-to-face with an election official. • All reasonable precautions shall be taken to ensure ballots cannot be duplicated or fraudulent ballots cast in an election. • The election results must be repeatable and reproducible by independent means.

    ONE MAN,ONE
